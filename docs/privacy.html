ReplyWise Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ReplyWise ("the App") is developed by NovaAI Studio.

Information We Collect

ReplyWise may collect the following information:

  • Anonymous device identifier
  • Anonymous application user ID
  • Messages that you submit for AI processing
  • App usage information
  • Subscription and purchase status

We do not intentionally collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location unless you explicitly provide them.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered replies
  • Polish and improve your writing
  • Explain the meaning and intent of messages
  • Verify subscriptions and purchased credits
  • Improve app reliability, security, and performance
  • Prevent abuse and fraudulent use of the service

AI Processing

When you request AI-powered features, the text you submit is securely transmitted to our AI service providers, such as OpenAI, solely for the purpose of generating the requested response.

We do not use your submitted content for advertising purposes.

Purchases

Subscriptions and credit purchases are processed through Google Play and RevenueCat.

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Data Storage

We store only the minimum information necessary to operate the service, including anonymous account information, usage records, and purchase and subscription status.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to help protect your information during transmission and storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

ReplyWise is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Last updated date.
